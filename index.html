About Me

Hello there! I'm Isabelle Ng, an undergraduate at UC Berkeley passionate about computer science, data science, and music. I enjoy finding ways to solve business problems with AI and exploring the potential of natural language processing.

My academic and professional journey includes data engineering and LLM development contract work with Mattel Inc. and Conagra Brands Inc., as well as interning on the engineering team at H2O.ai, where I worked on incorporating a chatbot designed to assist employees with queries about H2O.ai products. I am currently working at Accenture's Center for Advanced AI, focusing on researching and improving a text-to-SQL agentic framework to support client needs.

Outside of technology, I'm a lifelong musician who loves writing original songs. I also founded and led UC Berkeley's chapter of the International Software Product Management Association, teaching product management fundamentals and organizing global AI product summits. Having retired from presidency, I now focus on teaching Data C88C at Berkeley's College of Computing, Data Science, and Society as a head teaching assistant, and help nurture culture as a VP of Data Science Society, Cal's oldest student organization dedicated to data science and machine learning.
